About Us

Welcome to National Institute of Journalism

The Courses are designed to provide the learners with fundamental knowledge and practical skills in the field of journalism.

The short-term courses aims to equip the learners with the necessary skills to work as an entry-level journalists in various media platforms such as print,online and broadcast.

We are registered as the Affiliated Centre, and authorised by the Council for Vocational Education and Skill Development, licensed from Ministry of Corporate Affairs - Govt of India.
